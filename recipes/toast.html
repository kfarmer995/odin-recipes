Toast

Description

This is a paragraph that describes toast.

Toast is great. Put it in your mouth.

Ingredients:
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Cayenne Pepper
Steps:
  1. Put 7 pieces of bread on a plate
  2. Take them off the plate and put them in a 7 slot toaster

    3. If your toaster is too big, use more bread to fill it up
  3. Put butter and cheese on the toasted bread
  4. Eat the container of cayenne pepper
